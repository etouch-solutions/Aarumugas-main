Our Services

Comprehensive wedding decoration services to make your day perfect

Wedding Decoration

We specialize in traditional and modern wedding decorations including mandap setup, stage design, floral décor, lighting, entrance arches, and reception arrangements—crafted beautifully to match your vision and theme.

Reception Decoration

We create elegant and eye-catching reception setups with beautiful stage backdrops, floral arrangements, lighting, and customized couple seating. Every detail is designed to add charm and class to your celebration, making your special evening truly unforgettable.

Birthday Party Decoration

Make birthdays extra special with our vibrant and creative decorations. From themed backdrops and balloon arches to custom name boards and table setups, we design fun and festive environments for kids and adults alike—perfect for unforgettable celebrations.

Puberty Function Decoration

We design elegant and culturally rich setups for puberty functions, featuring traditional decor elements like floral arches, banana leaves, kuthu vilakku, and personalized stage backdrops. Our aim is to honor this important milestone with beauty, grace, and a touch of modern charm.

Function Hall Booking

We offer spacious, well-equipped function halls perfect for weddings, receptions, birthdays, puberty functions, and other special occasions. With elegant interiors, ample seating, and full decoration support, our halls ensure a comfortable and memorable experience for you and your guests.

Stage Decoration

We create stunning stage decorations that become the centerpiece of your event. Whether it's for a wedding, reception, or birthday, our designs include elegant backdrops, floral arrangements, draping, and lighting—perfectly tailored to your theme and celebration.



Chendai Melam

Add a touch of tradition to your celebration with our vibrant Chendai Melam performances. Perfect for wedding entrances, temple rituals, and grand processions, the rhythmic beats create an energetic and auspicious atmosphere that uplifts every moment of your special day.

Nathaswaram Package

Add a touch of tradition to your special occasion with our Nathaswaram Package. Perfect for weddings, temple festivals, and cultural events, this live instrumental ensemble creates an auspicious and soulful atmosphere. Featuring skilled artists on Nathaswaram and Thavil, this package brings authentic South Indian classical music to life, enhancing every ceremonial moment with divine melodies and vibrant rhythms.

Garlands (Maalai)

Elevate your wedding and traditional ceremonies with our beautifully handcrafted fresh flower garlands. Designed using premium blooms like jasmine, rose, and marigold, our garlands (maalai) symbolize love, purity, and prosperity. Perfect for weddings, engagements, poojas, and other sacred rituals, each garland is made to order with care and cultural significance in mind.

Flower Bouquets & Veni

Complete your traditional look and decor with our elegant flower bouquets and intricately crafted veni (hair garlands). Made from fresh, fragrant flowers like jasmine, rose, and tuberose, our bouquets add charm to bridal entries and ceremonial décor, while our venis beautifully enhance traditional hairstyles for brides, dancers, and devotees. Ideal for weddings, poojas, and classical performances.

Return Gifts

Show your gratitude with thoughtful return gifts that leave a lasting impression. Perfect for weddings, engagements, baby showers, and traditional functions, our curated selection includes eco-friendly items, spiritual keepsakes, handcrafted decor, and customizable gift sets. Each gift is beautifully packed to reflect warmth, appreciation, and the spirit of celebration.

DJ Services

we understand that your special day deserves a soundtrack as unique as your love story. Specializing in weddings, engagements, and milestone celebrations, our experienced DJs go beyond simply playing songs. We meticulously curate playlists, offer expert MC services to guide your event flow, and provide state-of-the-art sound and lighting to create the perfect ambiance. From romantic ceremony melodies to an epic dance party that keeps everyone moving, we work closely with you to ensure every musical moment reflects your vision and leaves an indelible impression on you and your guests.

Couple Entry

Make your entrance unforgettable with our stunning couple entry setups. We specialize in creating personalized and breathtaking entrances that reflect your unique love story. From floral arches to elegant drapery and captivating lighting, we work closely with you to design an entry that sets the tone for your celebration. Let us help you make a grand statement as you walk hand in hand into your new life together.

Catering Services

we believe every event deserves to be a culinary celebration. From intimate family gatherings to grand weddings, corporate functions, and traditional festivals in Karur and beyond, we bring delicious, meticulously prepared food directly to you. Our passion lies in crafting unforgettable dining experiences, blending authentic local flavors with diverse cuisines to suit every palate. With a focus on fresh ingredients, impeccable presentation, and seamless service, we ensure your guests are delighted and you can relax and enjoy your special occasion. Let us transform your vision into a delightful reality.

Honeymoon Packages

we believe every couple deserves a romantic getaway to celebrate their love. Our honeymoon packages are thoughtfully designed to provide you with unforgettable experiences, whether you prefer a serene beach escape, a cozy mountain retreat, or an adventurous journey exploring new cultures. We take care of all the details, from luxurious accommodations to intimate dining experiences, ensuring your honeymoon is everything you've dreamed of and more. Let us help you create beautiful memories as you embark on this new chapter together.
